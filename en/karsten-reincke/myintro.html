Short Introduction

For more than 20 years I have been working as a software developer. During the last 15 years I have also been acting as a IT Project Leader. Today I am engaged as a Senior Expert at  'Deutsche Telekom AG / Products & Innovation' where I work as a PMI certified Project Manager, Certified ScrumMaster, and Technical Project Leader. Currently I am a leading member of the Telekom Open Source Review Board and a main developer of the OSLiC, the (Telekom) Open Source License Compendium.

My commercial and private activities are accompanied by open source (software). As a little part of the large and great GNU/Linux community I am proud to be able to give ideas and software back to the community: a chargeback for the received world of free software.
