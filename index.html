Short Introduction

For more than 25 years I have been working as a software developer. During the last 15 years I have also been acting as a IT Project Leader.

After having worked as a Senior Expert at 'T-Online International AG' and 'Deutsche Telekom AG / Products & Innovation' during the last 12 years, I now focus on cloud technologies in 'Deutsche Telekom Technik GmbH'. Previously, I acted the part of a PMI certified Project Manager, a Certified ScrumMaster, a Technical Project Leader and an Open Source Compliance Manager, who wrote and maintained the (Telekom) Open Source License Compendium. Today - as part of a real agile team - it is a pleasure for me, to be back at the terminal and the keyboard: I am now linking the hardcore software development with the mindset of strategic project leadership and open source software compliance - for managing the issues of complex open stack orchestrations adequately.

So, my commercial and private activities are - again and still - accompanied by open source (software). As a little part of the large and great GNU/Linux community I am proud to be able to give ideas and software back to the community: a chargeback for the received world of free software.
